About Journal-ify

Our brand intends to provide our customers with the proper tools to ensure they fulfill their responsibilities while simultaneously maintaining their health.The site our company procured guarantees that our customers will be able to efficiently complete their tasks while enjoying the luxury of both creativity and the satisfaction of a job well done.

The themes which are wholly customisable allows the customer to choose a profile that they believe is aesthetically pleasing. The initiative to create a source the promotes balance between work and wellness.

Our Team

Franyi Paulino

Co-Founder

Franyi Paulino is soon entering her senior year in Yonkers Middle High School. She had decided to apply to the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program in order to become familiar with a field she had no prior experience in. After completing the program she was moved towards Bioengineering. Besides Coding, drawing and reading are also included in her list of interests. Which is why when given the opportunity had decided to create a digital bullet journal, a combination of all her passions alongside her team members Marina, Ophelia, and Natalie.
